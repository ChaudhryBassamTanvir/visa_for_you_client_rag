VISA PROCESS - VISA FOR YOU CONSULTANCY

GENERAL PROCESS FOR ALL COUNTRIES:
1. Choose university and program
2. Get admission offer letter
3. Show proof of funds
4. Apply for student visa
5. Get medical done if required
6. Biometrics appointment
7. Visa decision (4-12 weeks)

UK STUDENT VISA PROCESS:
- Apply online at UKVI
- CAS number from university required
- Funds: £1,334/month for London, £1,023 outside London (9 months)
- IHS fee: £776 per year
- Processing: 3 weeks

CANADA STUDY PERMIT:
- Apply online via IRCC
- Funds: CAD 10,000 + first year tuition
- Biometrics required
- Processing: 8-16 weeks
- SDS stream available for faster processing

AUSTRALIA STUDENT VISA (500):
- Apply via ImmiAccount
- Genuine Temporary Entrant (GTE) statement required
- Funds: AUD 21,041 per year
- OSHC health insurance required
- Processing: 4-6 weeks

GERMANY STUDENT VISA:
- Apply at German embassy/consulate
- Blocked account: €11,208 required
- Language skills proof
- University admission letter
- Processing: 6-12 weeks

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED (GENERAL):
- Valid passport (6+ months validity)
- Admission letter from university
- Academic transcripts and degrees
- English proficiency test (IELTS/TOEFL)
- Bank statements (last 6 months)
- Sponsor letter and documents
- Photographs
- Medical certificate (some countries)
- Police clearance certificate
