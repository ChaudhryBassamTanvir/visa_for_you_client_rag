SCHOLARSHIPS - VISA FOR YOU CONSULTANCY

FULLY FUNDED SCHOLARSHIPS:

1. Commonwealth Scholarship (UK)
- Coverage: Full tuition + living allowance + flights
- Eligibility: Pakistani/developing country citizens, min 3.0 CGPA
- Deadline: December each year
- Fields: All fields

2. Chevening Scholarship (UK)
- Coverage: Full tuition + living + flights
- Eligibility: 2 years work experience, leadership potential
- Deadline: November each year

3. DAAD Scholarship (Germany)
- Coverage: Monthly stipend €850-1200 + tuition
- Eligibility: Min 3.0 CGPA, German/English programs
- Deadline: October-November each year

4. Fulbright Scholarship (USA)
- Coverage: Full tuition + living + flights
- Eligibility: Pakistani nationals, excellent academics
- Deadline: February each year

5. CSC Scholarship (China)
- Coverage: Full tuition + accommodation + stipend
- Eligibility: Min 2.5 CGPA, any nationality
- Deadline: March-April each year

6. Australian Awards Scholarship
- Coverage: Full tuition + living allowance + flights
- Eligibility: Pakistani nationals, min 2.5 CGPA
- Deadline: April each year

PARTIAL SCHOLARSHIPS:

- UBC: International Leader of Tomorrow Award
