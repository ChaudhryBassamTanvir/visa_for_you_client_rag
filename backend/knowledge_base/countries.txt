STUDY ABROAD DESTINATIONS - VISA FOR YOU CONSULTANCY

UNITED KINGDOM:
- Top universities: Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial College, UCL
- Average tuition: £15,000-£35,000 per year
- IELTS requirement: 6.5 minimum
- Minimum CGPA: 3.0/4.0
- Part-time work: 20 hours per week allowed
- Post-study work visa: 2 years (Graduate Route)
- Average living cost: £12,000-£15,000 per year
- Popular courses: Business, Engineering, Medicine, Computer Science
- Visa type: Student visa (Tier 4)

CANADA:
- Top universities: University of Toronto, UBC, McGill, Waterloo
- Average tuition: CAD 20,000-45,000 per year
- IELTS requirement: 6.5 minimum
- Minimum CGPA: 2.5/4.0
- Part-time work: 20 hours per week allowed
- Post-study work permit: Up to 3 years (PGWP)
- Average living cost: CAD 15,000-20,000 per year
- Popular courses: Engineering, Business, IT, Healthcare
- Pathway to PR: Express Entry, PNP

AUSTRALIA:
- Top universities: Melbourne, Sydney, ANU, Monash
- Average tuition: AUD 25,000-45,000 per year
- IELTS requirement: 6.5 minimum
- Minimum CGPA: 2.5/4.0
- Part-time work: 48 hours per fortnight
- Post-study work visa: 2-4 years
- Average living cost: AUD 20,000-25,000 per year
- Popular courses: Engineering, Nursing, Business, IT

GERMANY:
- Top universities: TU Munich, Heidelberg, LMU Munich
- Average tuition: FREE at public universities (€500-1500 semester fee only)
- IELTS requirement: 6.0 (many programs in English)
- Minimum CGPA: 3.0/4.0
- Part-time work: 120 full days per year
- Post-study work visa: 18 months job search
- Average living cost: €10,000-12,000 per year
- Popular courses: Engineering, Computer Science, Natural Sciences

UNITED STATES:
- Top universities: MIT, Harvard, Stanford, Columbia
- Average tuition: $25,000-60,000 per year
- IELTS requirement: 7.0 minimum / TOEFL 90+
- Minimum CGPA: 3.0/4.0
- Part-time work: 20 hours on-campus only (F-1 visa)
- Post-study work: OPT 1 year, STEM OPT 3 years
- Average living cost: $15,000-25,000 per year
- Popular courses: Business, Engineering, Computer Science, Medicine

MALAYSIA:
- Top universities: UM, UPM, UTM, UTAR
- Average tuition: MYR 15,000-40,000 per year
- IELTS requirement: 5.5 minimum
- Minimum CGPA: 2.0/4.0
- Part-time work: Limited
- Average living cost: MYR 12,000-18,000 per year
- Best for: Low budget students, pathway to other countries
- Popular courses: Engineering, Business, Medicine

CHINA:
- Top universities: Tsinghua, Peking University, Fudan
- Average tuition: $3,000-10,000 per year
- IELTS requirement: 5.5 or HSK for Chinese programs
- Minimum CGPA: 2.0/4.0
- Scholarships: CSC Scholarship (full funded available)
- Average living cost: $3,000-6,000 per year
- Best for: Very low budget, fully funded opportunities
